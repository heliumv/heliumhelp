Sweet-Sour Tofu

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 10.5 oz pkg firm lite tofu
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 medium green bell pepper, seeded, -- cut in squares
  • 1/2 large onion, cut into 8 wedges
  • 8 ounces can pineapple chunks in unsweetened -- juice
  • 9 ounces jar sweet and sour sauce
  • 4 cups hot cooked rice
Directions:
Cut tofu into small bite-sized cubes; toss with cornstarch. Spray large nonstick skillet with nonstick cooking spray. Heat over medium-high heat until hot. Add tofu; cook 5 to 8 minutes or until golden brown. Place tofu on plate.
Add bell pepper, onion, and pineapple with juice to same skillet. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer uncovered 7 to 10 minutes or unti onion is soft and liquid has been reduced. Add cooked tofu and sweet nd sour sauce, stir gently to mix. Cover; cook 1 minute or until thickened and bubbly. Serve over hot cooked rice.
