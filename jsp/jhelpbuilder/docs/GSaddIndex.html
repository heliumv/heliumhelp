JHelpBuilder - Add Index

Now we will add an Index to our project.

To add , we go to the HelpSet tab.

  • Go to the HelpSet tab.
  • Click the button in the toolbar, and select the "New View" item.
  • A View Creation Wizard will popup. Choose javax.help.IndexView on the 1st screen.
  • Click on Next.
  • Enter a file name for your "Index" file. You can also use the "Browse" button to select a file.[index.xml]
  • Click on Next.
  • In this screen, enter the view Name, and Label.[Index, Index]
  • The information you entered is displayed. You can go back and change any value. Click "Finish" to create the Index.
  • The Index are now displayed in the HelpSet tab and Views tab.
