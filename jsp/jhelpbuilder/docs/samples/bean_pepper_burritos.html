Black Bean And Bell Pepper Burritos

Ingredients:
  • 1 can black beans
  • 3 bell peppers (different colors are best: red, yellow, orange, green, etc),chopped
  • 1 tsp (or to taste)dried garlic, or chopped garlic
  • cloves
  • Tortillas
  • Salsa
Directions:
Drain the can of black beans, reserving 1/4 cup of liquid to create a "sauce" if you like saucy burritos.
Combine beans, peppers, garlic, and salsa, throw mixture into a tortilla, and nuke for 30sec to 1min.
The bell peppers can be roasted for a richer flavor (over a low, open gas flame or on a cookie sheet under an oven broiler until outer skin is black; immerse in cold water & peel off outer skin WATCH OUT for the innards as they drain--they're HOT!)
