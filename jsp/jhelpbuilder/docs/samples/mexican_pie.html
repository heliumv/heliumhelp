Mexican Pie

Crust:
  • 1 1/2 cup corn meal
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 1 can creamstyle corn
  • 1 cup low fat soy milk

    • Mix till all is evenly moist Pour into 9X13 baking pan, spread evenly.
Filling:
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium zuchinni, chopped
  • 1 small hot pepper, minced (as much as you dare)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 cups cooked beans, whatever you have around
  • 2 cups tomato sauce

    • saute in wine or broth. Simmer till it is thick wonderful. Pour over crust
Topping:
  • about 16 ounces soft or silken tofu
  • 1/3 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • about 1/2 cup more or less water

    • Blend until smooth and the consistency of yogurt or sour cream, add water as required.
Directions:
Pour over filling and spread evenly. Sprinkle with paprika
Bake at about 375 deg F for about 1 hour.
Serve with a salad or a side of steamed veggies.
